## 2 — Fiscal calendar: debt ceiling, shutdowns, TGA mechanics, and flashpoints

[HARD: 2026-04 Treasury financial-statement note] The 2025 debt-limit episode is the template: Public Law 118-5 suspended the debt limit through 2025-01-01, Treasury began extraordinary measures on 2025-01-21, Public Law 119-21 raised the limit from 36.104 trillion dollars to 41.104 trillion dollars on 2025-07-04, and Treasury resumed normal operations on 2025-07-07 ([Treasury FY2025 Note 12](https://fiscal.treasury.gov/system/files/2026-04/2025-note-12-financial-statements.pdf)).  [HARD: 2025-03-26, CBO] CBO’s March 2025 debt-limit analysis estimated that extraordinary measures would probably be exhausted in August or September 2025, but it also flagged a downside timing risk in late May or June if borrowing needs were materially greater before June tax receipts and June 30 extraordinary measures arrived ([CBO Federal Debt and Statutory Limit, March 2025](https://www.cbo.gov/publication/60887)).  [HARD: 2026-06-14 endpoint test] With total public debt outstanding near 39.221 trillion dollars on 2026-06-11 and the statutory limit raised to 41.104 trillion dollars in July 2025, the 2026 status is not a live X-date episode in the same mechanical sense as early 2025, but the cushion should be monitored with debt-to-the-penny, weekly or daily debt-subject-to-limit materials if Treasury posts them, bill supply, and TGA balances ([Fiscal Data debt-to-the-penny API](https://api.fiscaldata.treasury.gov/services/api/fiscal_service/v2/accounting/od/debt_to_penny?sort=-record_date&page%5Bsize%5D=3), [Treasury FY2025 Note 12](https://fiscal.treasury.gov/system/files/2026-04/2025-note-12-financial-statements.pdf)).

[HARD: 2026-06-14 endpoint test; 2026-05-28 Cboe page] Cboe’s delayed quote API page is public, but the guessed CDN quote endpoints returned HTTP 403 in testing, and Cboe DataShop materials indicate live or delayed index prices require CSMi subscription in the All Access API context ([Cboe delayed quotes page](https://www.cboe.com/delayed_quotes/API/quote_table/), [Cboe All Access API](https://datashop.cboe.com/cboe-all-access-api)). [HARD: ICE Developer Portal] ICE public developer pages describe MOVE, DXY, and USD SOFR swap rate/swap-spread data as index or benchmark products delivered through ICE Connect, ICE feeds, ICE Data API, redistribution partners, or direct IBA licensing, so “free live” should not be assumed from page visibility ([ICE MOVE Index](https://developer.ice.com/fixed-income-data-services/catalog/ice-data-indices-move-index), [ICE Currency Indices](https://developer.ice.com/fixed-income-data-services/catalog/ice-data-indices-currency-indices), [ICE Swap Rate](https://developer.ice.com/fixed-income-data-services/catalog/ice-swap-rate)).

[DIR] Free aggregators such as Yahoo endpoints can be useful for internal QA because they returned DXY and gold-futures payloads in testing, but they should not be the production entitlement layer for a market-intelligence site without legal review and explicit redistribution rights.
