## The system laws these combos obey (codified from owner taste + the JCJ corpus)

1. **Cool/neutral desaturated dark ground · warm paper ink · soft-teal structure · gold-metal accent.** Canvases whisper their hue (never saturated blue, never pure black — "avoid all-black slides"). Inks are always warm (Ivory/Oat/Cream/Parchment family). Mains are soft teals/greens (Verdigris leading). Accents are gold metals (Brass/Old Gold/Desk Amber/JCJ Gold) — never orange-reds.
2. **Saturation-Footprint Law.** Footprint budget is inversely proportional to saturation — and includes type size. Bright yellow: ≤2 words, medium numerals, ≤2 elements. Muted metals: headline/hero scale allowed. ("Bright colors >5% coverage → amateur.")
3. **Tonal Ladder Law.** Same-family mains encouraged when ≥4.5:1 from the canvas (auto-family derivation targets ~6:1); near-depth same-family adjacency is mud (forbidden).
4. **Derived elevation, no shadows in-flow.** Surface = canvas + 3.5–8% lift (white or main-tint), raised one step further; hairline borders for data density, none for reading surfaces; top-highlight for raised feel; zebra for tables; shadows reserved for floating elements only.
5. **Adaptive text ramp.** Canvas luminance > 0.015 → strengthened ramp (body .80 / muted .61 / faint .45 mixes) so muted labels stay ≥4.5:1.
6. **One yellow-family member per context;** alert red quarantined to genuine live alerts/losses; Latent reality state is grey, never red.
