## 1 · FRICTION THEORY (Investor Persona System)

**Core thesis:** "Allocators don't reject strategies because of missing information — they stall, decline, or ignore because of unresolved friction." … "Capital flow starts with friction removal, not information delivery."

**The Three Core Truths:** 1. "Friction is emotional before it is rational" ("they say 'we need more data' but feel 'I don't feel safe moving forward yet'"). 2. "Friction accumulates silently" ("each unanswered doubt, each design misstep, each poorly timed ask stacks into disengagement"). 3. "Friction decides funnel entry" ("before performance, before custody, before fees — the allocator must feel it's safe to even look").

**Dual brain model:** Emotional Safety (limbic — "Will this make me look foolish or unsafe?": referral anxiety, reputational fear, loss trauma → hesitation/gatekeeping/avoidance) precedes Analytical Clarity (prefrontal — "Can I logically defend this?": custody trust, performance skepticism, ops complexity → detailed questioning). **"Until the allocator feels safe, logic doesn't matter."**

**Four pressure sources:** Psychological (loss/embarrassment/regret/career) · Social (colleagues/committees/family) · Organizational (bandwidth/mandates/compliance) · Category (industry fatigue, crypto skepticism). "Each allocator's persona is defined by how these pressures stack."

**8 Friction Families:** Inertia & Overwhelm · Complexity & Comprehension · Trust & Custody · Career & Reputational Risk · Social & Referral Optics · Timing & Macro Paralysis · Mandate & Structural Conflict · Trauma & Fatigue.

**Persona doctrine:** "Personas are not demographics — they are friction composites." A persona = behavioral signal map: primary hesitation · slide blocks + tone layers that reduce it · trusted format · appropriate CTA behavior. "This is not a pitch tool — it's a routing system."

**Softball-question doctrine:** "Softball questions are often emotional proxies" — every question reveals what they fear, where they are in the funnel, what tone/format/CTA feels safe.

## 5 · THE REFRAMING SYSTEM (Reframing Guide)

**7-element sequence schema:** Surface Signal (what they say) · Hidden Monologue (the real friction) · Persona-Specific Tone · Strategic Reframe (the cognitive bridge) · Tactical Truth Surfacing (the unlocking question) · Expected Reaction Pattern · Progressive Conversation Path (ends in a Never-rule).
**Core sequence:** "1 Immediate Validation — never argue with their concern · 2 Hidden Truth Acknowledgment · 3 Structural Bridge · 4 Peer Permission · 5 Progressive Path — enable movement without forcing."
**Triads:** friction layers Surface/Hidden/Core · reframe steps Validation/Reframe/Bridge/Evidence/Progress · persona needs **Safety/Status/Solution**.
**Friction Resolution Velocity:** surface=single conversation · structural=multiple touchpoints · emotional=extended nurturing · **trauma=infinite patience**.
**Tone matrix (segment level):** HNWI peer/ego-protective · FO legacy-minded/professional-warm · Institutional formal/precise/committee-conscious · Regional culturally-aware/relationship-first · **Damaged patient/zero-pressure/trauma-informed** · Analysts data-respectful/adversarial-ready.
**Reframe patterns by theme:** Comprehension → "you don't need to understand the underlying, only the discipline" (map to mastered asset class: SWIFT, commodities-futures-in-the-70s) · Trust/trauma → "don't trust — verify" (enumerate verifiable third parties; "we're not asking for trust, we're providing transparency") · Authority/politics → arm the gatekeeper, pre-wire committees ("selling to the system, not the person") · Identity/status → preserve dignity, reframe acting-now as leadership · Value/comparison → concede their metric, switch the scoring (Sharpe; "one year at 200% or seven at 38%?"); self-select honestly ("if you need higher octane, we're probably not the right fit").
**Truth-surfacing question bank (5 types — precedent / metric-substitution / fear-localization / self-confrontation / future-projection; 20 best banked in spec inputs; 200 total in raw).** Exemplars: "When you first invested in [their industry], what helped you get comfortable?" · "Is the career risk from the investment or how it's perceived?" · "What hurts more — the money lost or the trust betrayed?" · "Was your instinct wrong, or just your timing?" · "What question, if answered, would make you ready?"
**Never-rules (copy linter):** never argue with a concern · never mention DeFi/Web3 to traditionalists · never compete with the advisor · never diminish their trading success · never argue intrinsic value · never crypto-terminology on spouse routes · never pressure the Damaged segment (no countdowns, no scarcity) · never minimize FTX concerns.
**Material-format taxonomy:** one-pager · spouse edition · boardroom edition (w/ presenter notes, anticipated questions, video explainer) · board-ready deck · generation-tailored materials · data room (200+ docs) · CPA package (sample K-1s, CPA-to-CPA calls) · read-only API access · white-label education suite.
**Qualification protocols (the only pressure-licensed sequences):** Deal Tourist & Perpetual Researcher → staged disclosure, deadlines, question limits, "natural selection" exits.
**Ethic:** "The path to yes isn't through convincing — it's through understanding, validating, and enabling… not as script but as understanding."

## 7 · ADAPTATION CAUTIONS (for the spec)
1. **Layer assignment first.** Nothing ports to the PUBLIC layer unless it survives the mass-audience test: would this read as journalism/intelligence to a stranger, and is it safe to forward? Public layer borrows the *avoidance* systems (never-trigger rules, fear-avoided lists, share-safety doctrine, calm trust signals) and mass-appropriate adaptations (truth-surfacing questions as editorial interactives). Personas, routing, funnel CTAs, and promotion belong to the **506(c) fund section** (gated) and the off-site sales system only.
2. **Hex-bridge required:** routing tables use legacy hexes (#395D4F Custody Green, #C6EBC5 Mint, slide tone-tiers like #D8D3E4) — map to locked tokens via the duty-adaptation recipes; never import raw.
3. **Channel translation:** PDF/Gamma/Canva ≙ web equivalents (solo-scroll page / guided interactive / share-safe view). Suppression rules become display logic.
4. **Performance claims are fund-specific assertions** (38% CAGR, −22% vs −77%, provider names) — verify/parameterize before any use, and on the public layer treat ALL fund-performance content as fund-section-only pending compliance review.
5. **Pressure-prohibition is load-bearing:** the Damaged segment and GCC register forbid scarcity/urgency mechanics — these are LAWS in adaptation, not suggestions. On the public layer, pressure mechanics of any kind are out of scope entirely.
6. **The persuasion model is reassurance-and-timing**, never deception — the corpus' own fears-avoided lists ban "scarcity manipulation, sales closing tone, hype triggers." Undertone = peripheral calm + demonstrated competence, not trickery. The public layer's strongest persuasive asset is the QUALITY of the intelligence itself.
