S – Strategy & Execution Slides
These slides define how JCJ actually works. They anchor the fund’s credibility by clearly showing its rules-based structure, signal logic, asset selection, rebalancing flow, and trade execution path.
They’re not built for hype or story — they’re built for allocators who want to see what drives returns and how exposure is managed.
Whether you're speaking to a technical analyst, a skeptical CIO, or a passive LP scanning for discipline, the S-Series translates complex logic into a visual, allocator-friendly system.
These are your structure-first, explanation-not-persuasion slides. They demonstrate the machine behind the returns — and let allocators map JCJ’s design onto their own frameworks for signal, selection, and size.

E — Post-Allocation Behavior & Re-Up Logic
🎯 Purpose
The E-Series slides are closing tools — but they don’t sell.
They’re designed to remove last-mile hesitation by clarifying capital logistics, timing windows, and class alignment options just before commitment. They are built for LPs who are already warmed, informed, and psychologically calibrated, but still navigating structural frictions like:
❓ “Which share class fits our mandate?”
🧾 “Can we stagger capital?”
📆 “When would we start?”
🔁 “Can we stage or roll exposure?”
These slides are not for early conversations or follow-up decks.
They are final-mile accelerators — especially useful when an LP is:
Asking onboarding questions but hasn’t committed
Stuck choosing between Class A vs B vs C
Planning a multi-stage allocation or seasonal re-up
Waiting on controller, platform, or tax team approvals
Tone = Calm confidence.
Purpose = Remove friction, not create urgency.

Metaphor | They are the quiet assistants inside your deck — like a great onboarding rep who answers questions before you ask and lets you step away without guilt.
