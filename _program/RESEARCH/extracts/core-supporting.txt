Core & Supporting Colors
Rules:
Never use more than 2 non-neutral colors per slide outside Tier 1.
CTA buttons must use Tier 1/2 tones only; never red for CTA.
Always pass ADA contrast (≥4.5:1 for body text).
Blocked: neon greens, bright oranges, white text on yellow; no rainbow charts.
