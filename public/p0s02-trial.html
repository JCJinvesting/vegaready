When the world shakes, markets move first.

Vega — ν — measures how sensitive a position is to changes in volatility. Most investors absorb volatility; they react after the damage is done. VegaReady exists for the other kind: the reader who wants to see it coming.

We track global events, map how they cascade through markets, and publish the intelligence openly — the same intelligence JCJ Investing trades on, every day.

01The event

Everything starts with a shock.

Conflicts, sanctions, policy shifts, supply shocks — we catalogue them as they break, score them for market transmission, and timestamp every claim.
